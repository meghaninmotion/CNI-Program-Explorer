# CNI-Program-Explorer
A prototype mapping of CNI Program Plan themes and maturity

From early in Cliff Lynch's tenure as Executive Director, CNI published annual program plans at the fall CNI member meeting in which CNI established priorities and plans for the year to follow. That practice ended in 2019-2020 with the onset of the global COVID-19 pandemic. Historical CNI program plan views offer continuity of intractable challenges within the community and also emerging trends.

This visualization represents a slice of that evoluton of challenges, trends, and depth of engagement. It offers a view of seven cross-cutting themes over the final four years of CNI program publication from 2015/2016 to 2019/2020. Data were gathered from program plan PDFs and other details for each year, which are published openly on CNI's website. 

More details on the CNI Program Plans can be found at (CNI Program Plan Archive)[https://www.cni.org/program].

This visualization was developed by pairing with Claude AI to develop html to represent the movement of cross-cutting themes, and is presented for exploration. It can be further developed with your feedback and suggestions. Please share those thoughts - and report errors - as pull requests!
